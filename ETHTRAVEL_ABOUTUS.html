About Us

Welcome to Ethiopia Travel Buddy! I'm Liben Jatani - aka Leo, your personal tour guide and trusted travel companion. Join me for an unforgettable expedition as we delve into Ethiopia's hidden gems and rich cultures. Whether you seek thrilling adventures, historical insights, or serene natural escapes, I'm dedicated to crafting a personalized journey tailored to your desires.

With my passion for travel and extensive knowledge of the area, I'll lead you off the beaten path to discover the authentic essence of Ethiopia. From bustling markets and ancient landmarks to breathtaking landscapes and mouthwatering cuisine, every moment promises to be filled with excitement and wonder.

So, leave the details to me and let's create memories that will last a lifetime. Get ready to immerse yourself in the beauty and magic of Ethiopia with your dedicated guide, Leo. Your adventure begins here!
